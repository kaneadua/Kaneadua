CREATOR FAQ

How much does Kaneadua cost?


Our platform costs zero to use. You are free to create pages and upload your content without ever having to pay anything.



Should I form a corporation?


That is entirely up to you. Many other creators have found it to be beneficial to form a legal entity such as a corporation, but it depends on you as a creator and your situation. If in doubt, you may want to hire a legal adviser to get proper legal advice on the merits and costs of forming a legal entity. If you do plan to form a legal entity, you will need to do so before proceeding through the digital paperwork.



Can I enter an alias/nickname other than my legal name when I onboard?


No.You must enter your legal first and last name when onboarding. Do not enter an alias or nickname during the onboarding process.



What should I enter as my Company’s Legal Name?


You must enter the name of the legal entity that owns or has rights to publish the content, and is the legal entity that will be signing the Kaneadua Distribution Agreement. The company name you enter here must match the name as written on official documents with your bank and on Ghana Revenue Authority or United States IRS tax documents or foreign tax documents, if applicable.



Which name should I enter as my Company’s Legal Name if I’m an Independent Creator?


Do not enter your Doing Business As (“DBA”) or alias/nickname. Please make sure to enter your full legal name. The name you enter here must match the name as written on official documents with your bank and on Ghana Revenue Authority or United States IRS tax documents or foreign tax documents, if applicable.



How are payments handled? What about VAT & VAT MOSS?

Kaneadua never collects any sensitive payment information from buyers. Instead, it relies on third-party payment platforms to handle transactions securely. Kaneadua supports two payout models depending on your needs: Direct to you, where each purchase is a transaction to your Flutterwave, Paystack or Stripe account, and Collected by kaneadua.com, paid later. You can pick which mode you want to use from the seller settings page on your account. EU VAT (European Value-Added Tax) compliance is automatic when using Collected by Kaneadua, paid later. Direct payments involve a bit more work on your part, but kaneadua provides all the tools needed to do it.



What limits apply to projects and accounts?


We have set some limits for new account holders, to prevent abuse. By request we can disable the limits, all you have to do is to contact us.

  • There is a soft limit of 20 project pages.
  • Individual file size is also limited, and you should have 10 files max per page.The restriction can be raised to by request.

Kaneadua allow users to download files exactly as uploaded them you can upload any kind of project for any kind of platform. There are no restrictions. Kaneadua have classifications for games, comics, books, soundtracks, assets and tools.



Ownership of the contents uploaded


Creators fully own the contents. You are free to remove it at any time. You can read more on the terms of use. We just ask for the minimum number of rights necessary to run the site, such as permission to show your game page and host your game’s assets.



How does DRM (Digital Rights Management) work on our site?

Users download the games exactly as you uploaded them. No modifications are made to the files you upload unless requested.



Is 18+ content allowed on kaneadua?


There are no restrictions for the kind of content creators can host on kaneadua.com (assuming it’s legal). If you plan of collecting money for your content then you must adhere to the acceptable use policy of the respective payment provider:

  • Flutterwave
  • Paystack
  • Stripe



Can I give people access to my game without having them pay?


Yes. You can generate “download keys”. A download key is a special URL that gives a user full access to download your game without having to pay it. Creators use it for giving out copies of their game to bloggers or backers of a crowdfunding campaign.
You can track the number of times the key has been used, identify the key with a name, and revoke it if you think it is being abused.
